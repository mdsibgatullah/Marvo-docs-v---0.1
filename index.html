How to Install Mavro Theme

Install

  • Follow these simple steps to get started with your Mavro theme and transform your store into a professional online shop

    1. Common Steps

      Create a Shopify Partner Account

      Create Your Store

      Log Into Your Store

      Choose and Install the Mavro Theme

    2. Step 1. Install Form Shopify store

      From your Shopify dashboard

      go to Online Store (Themes)

      go to Popular Themes

      Click Visit Theme Store


      One---- Two---- Three----

    3. Step 2. Installation Manual

      Step 1: Download the Theme File

      Once you've purchased or acquired the Mavro theme, you'll receive a .zip file. Make sure to download and save it to your computer.

    4. Step 2: Upload the Mavro Theme

      On the Themes page, click the Upload Theme button located in the top-right corner. Choose the zip file you downloaded in Step 1. Click Upload and wait for the theme to upload to your store.


      One ------ Two ------
    5. Step 3: Publish the Mavro Theme

      Once the theme is successfully uploaded, it will appear in your theme library. To activate it, click on Actions next to the Mavro theme and then select Publish.


    6. Step 4: Customize Your Theme

      After publishing, go to Customize to begin personalizing your store. From here, you can adjust colors, fonts, layout, and other elements to match your brand. Use the intuitive drag-and-drop interface to make quick changes, or dive deeper into the theme's settings for advanced customizations.


    7. Step 5: Test Your Store

      Before you go live, make sure to preview your store on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices to ensure everything is working as expected.
